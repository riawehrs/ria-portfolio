Project

Toggle TV

Toggle TV is an android based app that enables users to stream movies to their TV. In March of 2014, I coded a web-based 1080p prototype to serve as an interactive demo of the app design.

Open Prototype

Preview of the web-based TV app prototype

Interactive Prototype

The Toggle prototype was to be used in upcoming meetings with prospective investors. As such, there was a fairly tight timeline and budget within which the interactive demo piece needed to be produced. We knew it would always be presented on a TV display, so it suited their purposes to have it be a fixed size.

One of the primary optional things I wanted to do but didn’t have time for was to implement infinite looping functionality for the slider. Seeing as this was one of my first front-end jobs, my skills have grown a lot since I worked on it. I’d love to go back to it sometime and make the slides loop seamlessly.
